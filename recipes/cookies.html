Peanut Butter Cookies

PB Cookies

Peanut butter cookies are a great with a cup of coffee or by themselves. Their not too sweet & are just the right consitency.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients thoroughly
  2. Roll dough into small balls and then press into cookies
  3. Bake until crispy
