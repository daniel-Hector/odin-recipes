Chocolate Chip Brownies

This 30 minute brownie recipe is both dilicious and fast!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups Flour
  • 1 cup Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, chocolate chips, and salt in a large bowl
  3. Spread the batter into pan
  4. Bake for about 20-30 minutes
  5. ALlow brownies to cool and enjoy!
