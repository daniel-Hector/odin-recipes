Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is a great dish. Its relatively easy to make and is very filling.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Fettuccine
  • Alfredo sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the Fettuccine according to package instructions
  2. Season chicken and pan fry. Then cut into 1/2 inch slices
  3. Simmer Alfredo sauce. While you wait, you can drain the Fettuccine
  4. Mix in the drained Fettuccine with the Alfredo sauce
  5. Serve Fettuccine with the chicken slices on top and garnish with parsley
